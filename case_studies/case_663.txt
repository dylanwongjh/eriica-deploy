User: I've been feeling overwhelmed and helpless as my mother's health continues to decline. I've tried to be there for her, but the emotional burden is becoming unbearable. I've read about caregiver burnout and how common it is, but I didn't think it would happen to me.

It's also crucial to remember that asking for help is not a sign of weakness but a necessary aspect of maintaining your own health and well-being.
