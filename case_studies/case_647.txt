Counselor: I can understand how frustrating this process can be for you. Lastly, I encourage you to practice self-compassion during this process. Decorating a room is an art, and it's normal to encounter challenges along the way. Be gentle with yourself and remember that the end result doesn't have to be perfect to be meaningful.

By focusing on collaboration, compromise, self-compassion, and recognizing the importance of your daughter's input, you'll likely find that the process becomes less stressful and more enjoyable for both of you.
